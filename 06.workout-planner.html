3-DAY FULL BODY SPLIT
Goal: Strength & Endurance

Perform 3 full-body sessions per week with at least one rest day in between (e.g., Mon, Wed, Fri).

Day 1 / Day 3
  • Squat
  • Bench Press
  • Barbell Row
  • Overhead Press
  • Plank
Day 2
  • Deadlift
  • Lunges
  • Pull-ups / Lat Pulldown
  • Dips / Triceps Extension
  • Bicep Curl
REST DAYS

Focus on active recovery (walking, light stretching) and ensure proper nutrition and sleep for muscle repair.

Note: Focus on 3-4 sets per exercise / 8-12 reps for hypertrophy (muscle gain).

4-DAY UPPER/LOWER SPLIT
Goal: Hypertrophy & Volume

Schedule your sessions as Upper, Lower, Rest, Upper, Lower, Rest, Rest (e.g., M, T, R, F).

UPPER BODY (A & B)
  • Bench Press / Dumbbell Press
  • Pull-ups / Lat Pulldown
  • Overhead Press
  • Barbell Row / Cable Row
  • Lateral Raises
  • Bicep Curls / Triceps Extensions
LOWER BODY (A & B)
  • Squats / Leg Press
  • Deadlifts / RDLs
  • Leg Extensions
  • Hamstring Curls
  • Calf Raises
  • Abs & Core

Note: Focus on 3-4 sets per exercise / 8-12 reps for hypertrophy (muscle gain).

5-DAY PUSH/PULL/LEGS SPLIT
Goal: High Volume Isolation

Cycle: PUSH (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps), PULL (Back, Biceps), LEGS, Rest, PUSH, PULL, Rest.

PUSH DAY
  • Flat Dumbbell Press
  • Incline Barbell Press
  • Shoulder Press (Machine/Dumbbell)
  • Cable Triceps Pushdown
  • Lateral Raises
PULL DAY
  • Weighted Pull-ups / Lat Pulldown
  • Seated Cable Row
  • Face Pulls
  • Shrugs
  • Hammer Curls
LEGS DAY
  • Squats
  • Leg Press
  • Leg Extensions
  • Seated Hamstring Curl
  • Calf Raises

Note: Focus on 3-4 sets per exercise / 8-12 reps for hypertrophy (muscle gain).
